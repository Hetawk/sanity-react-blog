2026 Call for PhD Applications

We are excited to announce that the call for applications to the PhD program 2026 of our Konrad Zuse School of Excellence in Reliable AI (relAI) is now open!

The novel, innovative PhD relAI program offers a cross-sectional training for successful education in AI including scientific knowledge, professional development courses and industrial exposure, providing a coherent, yet flexible and personalised training.

Funded applicants will receive a full salary for three years, including social benefits (TV-L E13 of the German public sector). They may receive additional support through travel grants for conference attendance, research stays, or home travel. Doctoral students are hosted by a relAI Fellow who helps them to define their research project. Depending on the affiliation of this hosting fellow, they enrol at TUM or LMU.

PhD Program
Our 2025 call for PhD positions, funded by relAI, is now open. The application deadline is January 13th, 2026 (23:59 AoE).

In general we accept students based on the following prerequisites:

Excellent master's degree (or equivalent) in computer science, mathematics, engineering, natural sciences or other data science/machine learning/AI related disciplines
a genuine interest to work on a topic of reliable AI covering aspects such as safety, security, privacy and responsibility in one relAIâ€™s research areas Mathematical & Algorithmic foundations, Algorithmic Decision-Making, Medicine & Healthcare, Robotics & Interacting Systems, or Learning & Instruction.
You need the following documents/information for starting the application process:

Your CV
Copies of your relevant diplomas and transcripts (in case a degree is still ongoing you can upload transcripts to prove you will have finished by the time you join relAI)
Short Motivation (max 1500 characters), also highlighting previous experience in the field
Choice of two preferred supervisors who are relAI Fellows, along with a short explanation (max 1000 characters) for each why the respective supervisor would be a good fit (illustrate with a thesis topic you would like to pursue if you have one in mind)
Names and addresses of two researchers who consent to be contacted for recommendation letters
Proof of English language proficiency: level C1 or equivalent 
(also acceptable: proof that University degree was taught mainly in English) 
The application process for associated PhDs, whose salary is funded by third parties, is regularly announced relAI internally (next time in early 2026).

MSc Program
Our 2025 call for MSc scholarships closed on June 17, 2025. Application reviewing is ongoing. Notifications for accepted candidates will begin in August, and waitlisted candidates may be contacted as late as September. All others will be notified in late September when the selection process has closed.
The 2026 call will likely open in May 2026.

Please note: relAI MSc students have to be inscribed in an MSc program in computer science, mathematics, engineering, natural sciences or other data science/machine learning/AI related disciplines at TUM or LMU, so make sure that you apply to that in parallel to the application to relAI. Most master application deadlines are end of May. Offers will be made subject to acceptance into such a program. So no need to worry if you are not accepted before submitting your relAI application.
